Our History

Our story begins in 1971 along the cobblestone streets of Seattle's historic Pike Place Market. It was here where Kylie's Cafe opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. Friends since their University of San Francisco days, Gerald Baldwin, a former English teacher, Gordon Bowker, a history teacher, were three 20 somethings who were passionate about the arts, fine food, good wine and, of course, great coffee
